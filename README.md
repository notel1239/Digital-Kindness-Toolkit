# Digital Kindness Toolkit

## Mission
A community-driven resource hub for Filipinos navigating online harassment, cyberbullying, and digital rights issues. Our goal is to empower individuals with tools, support, and information to foster a safer and kinder digital environment.

## Who This Is For
- Victims and survivors of cyberbullying, cyberbaiting, and online harassment
- Advocates, activists, and allies who want to support others
- Educators, parents, and community leaders seeking resources on digital safety
- Anyone interested in promoting digital kindness and ethical online behavior

## How You Can Get Involved
We believe in the power of community collaboration. You can contribute by:
1. **Suggesting new resources** – share links to helpful websites, hotlines, legal aid, mental health support, etc.
2. **Sharing personal stories** – anonymously or publicly, to inspire hope and solidarity.
3. **Proposing campaign ideas** – for social media movements, awareness drives, and educational materials.
4. **Reviewing and improving existing content** – through pull requests and constructive feedback.

## Quick Start
1. Read our [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md) to understand how to contribute.
2. Check out the [Issues](https://github.com/notel1239/Digital-Kindness-Toolkit/issues) to see what needs to be done.
3. Fork the repository, make your changes, and open a pull request.
4. Join the conversation and help build a kinder digital Philippines!
